üõ†Ô∏è JCR and Radio Troubleshoot


        
ASIP:

        
    
          
  • Is the ASIP in the Right Location (Radio 1 = Bottom Mount, Radio 2 = Top Mount)?
    • 
          
  • Is the ASIP power on? (VAA power must be on)
    • 
          
  • Is ASIP on correct hopset? Look under ADMIN/MISC on FBCB2.
    • 
          
  • Does ASIP time match DAGR time?
    • 
          
  • Is ASIP on CH 1?
    • 
          
  • Is ASIP in FH mode?
    • 
          
  • Does ASIP have COMSEC? Is it the correct fill?
    • 
          
  • Have you done a check on FM voice?
    • 
          
  • Are the antennas screwed down on mounts? Antenna cables correctly connected? Are antennas still tied down?
    • 
          
  • W2 cable from Radio 1 to Power Amp (PA) on radio mount (VAA)?
    • 
          
  • Charged HUB battery in ASIP radio?
    • 
        


        
CABLES:

        
    
          
  • Are all cables clean and serviceable?
    • 
          
  • Are cables connected between CPU ‚Üî screen, KGV-72, power cable and SIAD?
    • 
          
  • DAGR ‚Üî Antenna cable, power cable and SIAD (J2 port)?
    • 
          
  • Transceiver ‚Üî KGV-72 and PMA?
    • 
          
  • Screen ‚Üî keyboard and CPU cable?
    • 
          
  • PMA ‚Üî DAGR, KGV-72 and Transceiver?
    • 
        


        
DAGR:

        
    
          
  • Does DAGR have COMSEC fill?
    • 
          
  • Is DAGR data cable correctly attached to DAGR in J2 port?
    • 
          
  • Is the DAGR power cable attached?
    • 
          
  • Is the DAGR antenna attached to DAGR?
    • 
          
  • Is the DAGR antenna (puck) underneath equipment or radar scattering camouflage netting?
    •
